“I want a job… but what do I even put on a resume?”

Most students hit the same wall. You want a part-time job, but you've never had one — so the resume feels impossible. What counts as experience? How do you describe helping out at home or volunteering without it sounding made up? And when the application asks “Why do you want to work here?”, a one-line answer isn't going to cut it.

HireReady exists to fix exactly that. We take what you've actually done — school, clubs, sports, volunteering, chores, family responsibilities — and turn it into a resume and application answers that sound professional and real.
